# CareerCompass
CareerCompass AI is an intelligent career guidance platform designed to help students and early professionals discover the right career path based on their skills, interests, and market demand.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
Many students struggle to choose the right career due to lack of clarity, guidance, and mentorship.  
Despite having multiple opportunities, they are unable to align their skills with suitable job roles, leading to confusion and unemployment.

This project aims to solve this problem by providing AI-powered career suggestions, personalized roadmaps, and real-time guidance.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔐 User Authentication (Login / Signup)

- 📄 Resume Analyzer  
  - Upload CV  
  - Extract skills  
  - Suggest suitable job roles  

- 🤖 AI Career Discovery  
  - Analyze user interests + skills  
  - Suggest best-fit career paths  

- 🧪 AI Mock Interview  
  - Practice interview questions  
  - Get feedback  

- 🗺️ Personalized Roadmap  
  - Weekly learning plan  
  - Skill-building guidance  

- 📊 Progress Tracking  
  - Track learning journey  
  - Set goals and reminders  

---
